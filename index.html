How it works

1) Challenge

User receives a short, randomized gesture prompt. We capture a 10-second liveness video, device info, SIM/app IDs, coarse location, and timestamp.

2) Sign & hash

Evidence is signed and hashed. A tamper-evident record is created; hashes are batched for anchoring to provide a durable audit trail.

3) Verify

Relying parties (e.g., telcos, MFIs) verify the record when a transaction is approved, blocked, or disputed—reducing ambiguity and fraud.
